TOP 10 BEST DOG BREEDS TO OWN

1. Starting with the Golden retriever

One of the reason Golden retrievers are so popular is because of their trainability, to able to pick new tricks fairly easily.

Another great reason is for their happy and mellow nature, combined with their cuteness, makes them one of a kind.

2. Another best is Australian Shepherd

Australian Shepheard are well trained, easy-going, and very playful with kids and other dogs.
